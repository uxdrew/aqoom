Welcome!

Healthy living ain't always easy. Not all of us can run 3 miles a day and lay off the cold beers. Our aim isn't to make you stop doing what you love, it is to help make you aware of your habits - and in turn help you accumulate points you can turn into cash and also help give your insurance provider, general practicioner, health worker, and family, insight into your newly found healthy habits.
